  • {% blocktrans %}Quantity of parameters from different organizations may not be the same because some parameters are relevant only for several organizations.{% endblocktrans %}
  • {% blocktrans %}Within estimate absent may be such criteria as Fullness, Actuality, Navigation accessibility, HTML accessibility, File accessibility, Graphical accessibility as they may be not relevant for parameter.{% endblocktrans %}
  • {% blocktrans %}Parameter type can have two values: 1) "other" for formulated by experts parameters; 2) "npa" for formulated by normative legal acts parameters.{% endblocktrans %}
  • {% blocktrans %}Version of parameter's estimate can have two values: 1) "initial" for parameter's estimate before interaction of organization's representatives with experts; 2) \"default\" for final parameter's estimate after interaction of organization's representatives with experts.
